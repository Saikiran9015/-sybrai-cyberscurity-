Zero-Tolerance Ransomware Defense

Shielding Critical Infrastructure from Crypto-Locking Attacks

Our solution provides enterprise-grade protection, securing official domains, hospitals, and civic offices against sophisticated email-borne threats, phishing, and crypto-locking malware.

Layer 1: Proactive Prevention

  • **Anti-Spoofing (DMARC/SPF/DKIM):** Hardening email domains against phishing and impersonation.
  • **Zero-Day Malware Prevention:** Next-generation endpoint protection blocking unknown threats.
  • **URL & Attachment Sandboxing:** Isolating and analyzing malicious content before delivery.
  • **Behavioral Monitoring:** Detecting early signs of unauthorized encryption processes.

Layer 2: Incident Mitigation

  • **Network Micro-Segmentation:** Limiting lateral movement of malware inside the network.
  • **Automated Threat Hunting:** Rapidly identifying and isolating compromised endpoints.
  • **Hospital Data Shielding:** Specialized protocols to protect sensitive healthcare records (HIPAA/GDPR compliance).
  • **24/7 Threat Intelligence:** Continuous feeding of global ransomware indicators of compromise (IOCs).

Layer 3: Recovery & Continuity

  • **Encrypted, Immutable Backups:** Ensuring restore points cannot be tampered with by attackers.
  • **Rapid Restore Procedures:** Minimizing downtime and ensuring business continuity post-attack.
  • **Forensic Analysis:** Detailed reporting on the attack vector and attacker identity (where possible).
  • **Post-Incident Hardening:** Implementing immediate changes to prevent recurrence.
