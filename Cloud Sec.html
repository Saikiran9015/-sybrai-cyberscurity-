Vulnerability & Cloud Security

Deploying Zero-Trust Architecture Across Public Clouds

Our deep-dive penetration testing and security hardening services secure your digital assets from the foundation up. We address vulnerabilities in both traditional APIs and complex cloud native environments like AWS and Azure.

Deep Assessment & Pentesting

  • **API Penetration Testing:** Automated and manual checks for critical OWASP Top 10 risks.
  • **Web Application Audits:** Full-stack analysis of public-facing portals and e-governance platforms.
  • **Network Vulnerability Scanning:** Identifying weaknesses in perimeter defense and internal networks.
  • **Zero-Day Protocol Checks:** Testing resilience against unpatched and emerging attack vectors.

Cloud Hardening & Compliance

  • **Cloud Configuration Review:** CIS benchmarks for AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud security settings.
  • **Identity & Access Management (IAM):** Least-privilege principle enforcement and role auditing.
  • **Data Encryption at Rest & In Transit:** Ensuring compliance and protecting citizen data with strong encryption standards.
  • **Infrastructure as Code (IaC) Security:** Auditing Terraform/CloudFormation templates before deployment.

Actionable Risk Reporting

  • **Risk Scoring Matrices:** Quantifying threats with industry-standard CVSS scores and business impact.
  • **Prioritized Remediation Plans:** Clear, step-by-step guidance for fixing identified flaws.
  • **Secure Citizen Data Protection:** Specialized reporting focused on government and public data privacy needs.
  • **Post-Remediation Verification:** Re-testing to ensure all vulnerabilities have been successfully closed.
